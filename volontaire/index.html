What is Volontaire?

Volontaire is a web application designed to help non-profits manage their volunteer database. No need for downloads - simply register and
use Volontaire to further your cause right away. It is quick and easy to use.

Organize

Post opportunities, view activity schedules, store online documents, and allow volunteers to register through the online portal.

Features

Recruit

By using the online portal, fill short-term and long-term volunteer positions with user-friendly application forms.

Features

Communicate

Post announcements, share photos, and send emails. Widen your organization's impact by staying in touch with your volunteers.

Features

Analytics

Keep track of your department's performance by looking at key statistics with data-driven graphics. No need to spend hours on spreadsheets!

Features

Customization

Create and customize your organization's online portal with stunning visuals. Non-profit branding to the next level.

Pricing
