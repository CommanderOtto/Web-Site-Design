About Us

Company History

N&M Systems is the parent company of Volontaire. Headquartered in Orlando, FL, N&M Systems is dedicated to provide the latest technologies to serve the needs of your organization. Starting as a small company run by two brothers in 2016, it has quickly expanded to become a leader in web development for numerous kinds of systems. N&M Systems currently has offices in Indianapolis, Orlando, Miami, Sao Paulo (Brazil), Sevilla (Spain), Nice (France) and Milan (Italy).

What is Volontaire?

Volontaire is a web-based volunteer management software. With Volontaire, you can easily organize your institution's most important assets: People. Learn how you can improve your community outreach and impact with our software!
